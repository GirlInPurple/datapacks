Cublak View

Summary

With connections to The Borderlands from the road to the south and Middle Spawn and Spawn City [3] from the Jungle and Oak lines respectively, it could be considered one of the 3 suburbs of the main city; the other two being World Origin Road [2] and The Borderlands [1]. It contains a mountain with a glowing cube on top, a small plains village to the east, and a dual railway passing straight through the city; all with the theme of glowing purple crystals. FRTA Road 4 passes through the town, heading to Yuri's town. As of Late July 2023, construction of Cublak is paused.

The Siege of Spike Hill
The first major battle between the two factions, the newly formed "Vietnam", and a faction soon to be called "The RWA" (stands for Realm Wide Assassins) was fought over 17 slimeballs.
Granted we were pretty easy targets hunkered down in a small shack in an icespikes biome, but we did not need to have our base irreparably griefed because we wanted payment. bmbxbby, who was the one who initially started the entire conflict between the two factions, just didnt want to pay and instead said "i pay with your blood on my blade" and it caused the entire faction to flee northward to find a jungle, our new home.

Gameflame saw through this and promptly kicked him out of the newly formed RWA; he then took refuge in our jungle bunker. There were pros and cons to this.
Pros:

The RWA knows our location and has dropped 3 wither nukes on us, confirmed by video on 17/06/2021.
That was the very tiny peak of Vietnam's reign over the jungle

Because the jungle was so far out from spawn, around 15km, once our beds were destroyed we were doomed to respawn far away from the ruins. Thankfully i survived the raid and was able to last until they left, but our base was a bomb crater at that point, so I had two options; kill myself and respawn in enemy land, or stay and build from the ground up again.
The season ended shortly after.

Greater Spawn City Metropolitan Area

Towns

The Greater Spawn City Metropolitan Area (called SCM for Spawn City Metro from now on on this page) contains the towns of;

  • Spawn City - The main and largest city of the region, also the spawn point for new players
  • Cublak View - A mountain and small village just outside The Borderlands and on the border of the SCM
  • The Borderlands - A small neighborhood on top of Mount Colina
  • HumanBeingA1's Unnamed Town [no page] - The newest town in the region, a small house just south of Spawn City
  • World Origin Road - An abandoned town just south-east of downtown Spawn City

Location

Ocean Plains

Names

This town was known under 3 different names, here they are listed;

  • Ocean Plains: This was the name of the canceled FRTA Metro Station to go to this area; it has since become the common name for this region. It was supposed to be the second station on what was then the Mushroom Line but now would be on the Spruce Line.
  • Golden Bay City: The original name of the city, the town being meant to be the richest and most prosperous town on the server.
  • Golden Knights Castle: The name of the only remaining building residing in its borders, the ruins of the old castle of the Golden Knights.

History

Construction: Sometime in Early May 2023, @DJzombiehunter and @RainForCraft started construction on the castle for a failed group called The Golden Knights.

Deconstruction: Due to @RainForCraft leaving the server, possibly permanently, me and...

Revival: @ToxicTrapper has expressed interest in gaining ownership of this base, and it will be granted whenever he is ready.

Location

CaveTown

Inhabitants

All but two players on the server lived in this town, the excluded are @tacco2009 and @squeezal. The total population peaked at 4, with @DJzombiehunter, @TipDuy3010, @TrefleCrystal, and @zacrny all living in the cave.

Location and Surrounding Regions

CaveTown is, unironically, built almost entirely in a cave, with only a small map wall, a single house, and ~0.2km of paths outside of said cave. The cave is a lush cave, around 40 blocks deep near city center and 50 blocks deep at the lowest. [2]. The surrounding regions are a snowy, regular taiga, and massive iceberg biome just off the coast.

@AdaxJosue

@AdaxJosue is one of the prominent and consistent players in the Final Republic server. He has been an active participant in multiple seasons and has left a significant impact on the server's community. Known for his strategic gameplay and friendly demeanor, AdaxJosue has been involved in various alliances, rivalries, and town creations.

Biography:

@AdaxJosue joined the Final Republic server during its early days and quickly became an integral part of the community. Throughout the seasons, he has shown a keen interest in town building, resource gathering, and PvP activities. He is admired for his ability to navigate the server's complex political landscape and maintain diplomatic relations with different players and towns.

Contributions:

As a skilled builder, @AdaxJosue has been involved in the creation and development of several towns. Some of his notable contributions include:

  • Phoenixville: @AdaxJosue founded Phoenixville, a town known for its impressive architecture and thriving community. It served as a hub for trade and commerce, attracting players from all around the server.

  • The Great Expedition: @AdaxJosue participated in The Great Expedition, an ambitious project aimed at exploring uncharted territories and establishing new outposts across the map. This expedition led to the discovery of valuable resources and untapped potential in the server's world.

Character Traits:

Beyond his in-game achievements, @AdaxJosue is known for his helpful and supportive nature. He has mentored and guided new players, ensuring they have a smooth start in the server. His dedication to the Final Republic community has earned him respect and admiration from fellow players.

Trivia:

  • He is skilled in various aspects of the game, including redstone engineering, architecture, and resource management.
  • @AdaxJosue's diplomatic skills have played a crucial role in maintaining peace and cooperation among different towns and factions.

Conclusion:

@AdaxJosue's journey in the Final Republic has been filled with accomplishments and camaraderie.
