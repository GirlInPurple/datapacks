Baystone

Baystone is a player-made location in our server, serving as a record-keeping area. The name "Baystone" is a combination of "Beta" and "Stone," reflecting the beta-style and pixel art-based builds popular during the 2010-2015 era.

Residents

  • Founder: @Baystone

No maps are available at the moment, as the location is private.

Trivia

The name "Baystone" is pronounced "Be-Stone," reflecting its unique blend of beta-style and stone-based builds.

Granted we were pretty easy targets hunkered down in a small shack in an icespikes biome, but we did not need to have our base irreparably griefed because we wanted payment. bmbxbby, who was the one who initially started the entire conflict between the two factions, just didnt want to pay and instead said "i pay with your blood on my blade" and it caused the entire faction to flee northward to find a jungle, our new home.

Trivia

The original spelling of the name is "finalrepublic" but spelling it "final republic" is perfectly fine as people will get what you're saying.
The game series Final Fantasy was made as Square Enix's final attempt at a popular game, and managed to make it work. Final Republic's name is for a similar reason, this was intended to be my last fully public server before closing it all down, and then the server exploded in popularity, casuing the whole of FR S2 to set into motion.
